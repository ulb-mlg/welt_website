TrafficTwin

Months 12–36 · Mobility Digital Twin for Smart Cities

Overview

TrafficTwin is a mobility Digital Twin for smart cities that integrates traffic simulation, model calibration, multi-step-ahead forecasting, and 3D visualization. Using simulators like SUMO and MatSim, TrafficTwin enables what-if scenario analysis for urban planning and traffic management.

The system builds on related projects (TORRES, TULIPE, MOBIAID) and applies the EDITS methodology — from data assimilation to causal reasoning — to understand and predict urban mobility patterns.
