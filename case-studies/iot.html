WP6.1

Small-Scale IoT Devices

Months 1–12 · Controlled environment for DT pipeline development

Overview

The IoT case study serves as the entry point for EDITS validation. Using small-scale, controllable devices, the team develops and tests the core Digital Twin pipeline — from data collection and physics-based model calibration to surrogate learning and forecasting — before scaling to more complex urban and energy systems.

Three demonstrators are used:

  • Arduino-based robotic arm with EMG sensor control — demonstrating real-time DT interaction
  • Small wind turbine mockup — for testing calibration and forecasting pipelines
  • Smart farm setup — IoT sensor network for environmental monitoring
