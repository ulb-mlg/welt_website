Case Studies

Three progressively complex real-world Digital Twin applications validating the EDITS methodology

WP6.1

Small-Scale IoT Devices

Months 1–12

Arduino-based robotic arm with EMG sensors, small wind turbine mockup, and smart farm setup. These small-scale Digital Twins serve as a controlled environment for developing and testing the core DT pipeline before scaling to more complex systems.

Explore IoT case study →
WP6.2

TrafficTwin

Months 12–36

A mobility Digital Twin for smart cities, integrating traffic simulation (SUMO/MatSim), model calibration, multi-step-ahead forecasting, and 3D visualization. TrafficTwin enables what-if scenario analysis for urban planning and traffic management.

Explore TrafficTwin →
WP6.3

EnergyTwin

Months 19–48

A wind farm Digital Twin with real-time data integration, wind power forecasting, maintenance prediction, and counterfactual analysis of failures. Developed in collaboration with Università del Sannio (Italy) using ENET-RTLab simulation tools.
